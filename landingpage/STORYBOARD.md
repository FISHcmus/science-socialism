## Dark/Light Theme Breaks

The page is predominantly **light** (cream `#FAFAF7`), with two dark sections creating visual rhythm:

1. **Navbar** — dark bar anchored at top
2. **Section 7 (Video Theater)** — full-width dark break, cinematic feel
3. **Footer** — dark bar closing the page

This creates a sandwich: light → dark (video) → light → dark (footer).

## Scroll Animation Plan

| Section | Animation | Trigger |
|---|---|---|
| Hero | Title slides in diagonally (Constructivist), accent line sweeps | Page load |
| Stats | Numbers count up, cards stagger in | 20% visible |
| Chapters | Cards cascade in from left, staggered | 20% visible |
| Objectives | Left panel slides in, right panel follows | 20% visible |
| Timeline | Line draws left-to-right, dots pop in sequence | 20% visible |
| Exercise | Quote box fades in, tier cards stagger up | 20% visible |
| Video | Theater section has no entry animation (always visible when scrolled to) | - |
| Members | Cards cascade in rows | 20% visible |
| Discussion | Quote slides in from right | 20% visible |
| Resources | Cards fade in with stagger | 20% visible |
| CTA | Buttons pulse gently on arrival | 20% visible |
