## Key Lyrical Themes

- Honoring sacrifices of previous generations
- Celebrating Vietnam's sovereignty and freedom (Trường Sa, Hoàng Sa)
- Youth's responsibility to continue the peace
- National pride (flag, homeland, sunrise imagery)
- Unity and collective aspirations
